Privacy Policy for Super.Simple.Counter

Effective Date: 12.07.2025

Super.Simple.Counter is a minimal, offline-only counting app. We value your privacy and are committed to being fully transparent. This privacy policy outlines how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection

Super.Simple.Counter does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data created within the app (such as counters, names, colors, and counts) stays entirely on your device.

2. Analytics

This app does NOT use analytics tools or trackers of any kind.

3. Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers.

4. Permissions

The app only uses local device storage (SQLite) to store your counter data. No other permissions are requested.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, an updated version will be made available on the GitHub repository or the App Store listing.

6. Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact support by creating an issue at:
https://github.com/LeChef/Super.Simple.Counter./issues

Thank you for using Super.Simple.Counter!
